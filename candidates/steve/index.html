Steve Warner

Vic Director

Get to know me!

Hello Victorians! I’m Stephen Warner, and I’ve called Victoria College home for the last two years. Having written for The Varsity and The Strand, and spent my spare time attending UTSU meetings, I am keenly aware of the issues faced by Vic students and the UTSU. As your representative, I want to work to bring Student Union services East of Queen's Park, by collaborating with VUSAC on events and issues, and tailoring those events to the needs, desires, and lifestyles of Victoria College students. On March 24- 26, vote Stephen Warner for a BRIGHTER Victoria College!
